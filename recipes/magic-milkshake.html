Magic Milkshake

The best homemade milkshake you'll be making regularly! (No icecream!)

Ever want a milkshake but don't have any icecream in the freezer? This milkshake is delicious and extremely easy to make. I'm certain you'll never want to make it with ice cream again!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup powedered milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • a dash of vanilla
  • a dash of salt
  • 1 or 2 trays of ice (the more the better)

Steps:

  1. Place all ingredients into a large blender
  2. Blend for about 2 minutes

And that's it! Super easy, super quick, super delicious. And you can totally customize it. You can add a banana for a funky monkey! You can replace the cocoa with strawberries! Add some peanut butter! Throw in some candy canes! The possibilites are limitless!
